 Develop REST WS application for following business case.
 
 A bank, identified by a unique code, has branches at various locations. Every branch is identified by a unique branch code. Bank supports following types of accounts
	Saving Account
	Current Account
	Fixed Deposit Account
	Recurring Deposit Account
 
 A bank has back office (BO) and front office  (FO) functionalities.
 FO deals with customers for following
	Create customer
	Update KYS
	Opening of account
	Closing of account
	Withdraw money
	Deposit money
	Request Cheque book
 
 
 BO deals with back end function
        Print cheque  books
        Manage own Employees
        Create Employee
        Update Employee
        Transfer Employee from one branch to other branch.
